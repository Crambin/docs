<h1>Toggle Speak</h1>
Enables bot response to <strong>all</strong> messages by you in the channel this command is used in, similar to the <strong>speak</strong> command.<br>


Usage:  <code>toggle_speak</code><br>
aliases: <code>toggle_talk</code>, <code>talk</code>, <code>toggle</code>

Example:<br> 
<code>!toggle_speak</code> Will inform you of whether the bot will or will no longer respond to you in the channel this command is used in.<br>
When toggled on: "I will now respond to you in this channel."<br>
When toggled off: "I will no longer respond to you in this channel."
